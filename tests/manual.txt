>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>>MANUAL FOR USING TEXT EDITOR<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<<;
All the functions are same as usual text editor except a few....;
At any point of time, if you get stuck, press F1 key to open help menu...;
To copy/cut a word, move to the starring of the word and press Ctrl + C/X key...;
To copy/cut a line, move to the ending of the line and press Ctrl + C/X...;
To paste press..Ctrl+V key;
